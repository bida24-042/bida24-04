Welcome to Makgadikgadi Riverview Guest House

Nestled along the peaceful banks of the Chobe River in Kasane, Botswana, our guest house offers a unique landscape where the beauty of nature meets the timeless spirit of rock art. Located near the world-famous Chobe National Park, our guest house is the perfect base for wildlife adventures, river cruises, or simply soaking in the tranquil rhythm of the river.

Our Location - A Riverside Haven in Chobe

Surrounded by lush greenery and just a few minutes from Chobe's iconic wildlife, our setting is both serene and full of life. Wake up to the call of birds and watch elephants along the river banks. Whether you're a nature lover, cultural explorer, or simply in search of peace, Kasane offers it all - and Makgadikgadi Riverview Guest House places you right at its heart.
